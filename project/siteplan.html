Site Name

Héctor Miguel Castro Chávez

I chose this name for this assignment because "Eterna Primavera" suggest pleasant water mixed with outdoor activity and a welcoming environment (all of them appropriate for cycling.)

Optional domain availability: ebikeclubeternaprimavera.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a space where people can learn about and join an e-bike community. The site will include information about different bike routes, how to become a member, and exclusive group rides. It will also explain the benefits of using e-bikes and encourage people to explore outdoor activities in a fun and eco-friendly way.

Scenarios

  • What are the available e-bike routes, and which one is best for a beginner?
  • How can I join the E-Bike Club Eterna Primavera, and are there any requirements or fees?
