The Land of Eternal Spring

Morelos is known for its perfect year-round climate, earning it the nickname "La Tierra de la Eterna Primavera" (The Land of Eternal Spring). With temperatures averaging 20-25°C (68-77°F), every day is perfect for an e-bike adventure.

From the mystical archaeological sites to charming colonial towns, from lush forests to volcanic landscapes, Morelos offers incredible diversity in a compact area - perfect for exploring on an e-bike.
